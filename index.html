02 — About

Design that ships.
Code that performs.

I’m Patrick — a creative developer and product designer working at the intersection of brand, interface, and engineering. For the past eight years I’ve helped startups and studios turn ambiguous ideas into polished, high-performing products.

I care about the details most people never notice — the easing curve on a hover state, the 40ms that separates “fast” from “instant”, the sentence of microcopy that makes a flow feel effortless.

  • Craft first. Pixel-level precision, production-level pragmatism.
  • Motion with meaning. Animation that guides, never decorates.
  • Measure everything. Design is a hypothesis; data is the review.
